About Zenetrix

A collective of developers, thinkers, and creators striving for excellence in computer applications and academic leadership.

Who We Are

We are the BCA A batch (2025–2029) of Marian College Kuttikkanam (Autonomous). Our class name, Zenetrix, signifies our drive to push limits and build solutions that make a difference.

Our Vision

To cultivate academic rigour, technological innovation, and solid leadership skills, preparing each student to excel as a competent professional in the global tech ecosystem.

Learning Culture

We combine core computational theories with hands-on labs, workshops, peer mentoring, and hackathons, building a strong self-learning and collaborative environment.

Collaboration & Leadership

From organizing cultural fests and class projects to leading campus environmental audits, we believe that true education includes teamwork and active service.

BCA @ Marian

Discover the academic ecosystem, laboratories, and support structures at Marian College Kuttikkanam (Autonomous).

Autonomous Institution

Curriculum design aligned with international standards, fast evaluation systems, and specialized industry-led training modules.

Industry-Oriented Curriculum

Focuses on advanced software development, cyber forensics, web technologies, and database design, adapting quickly to technology shifts.

Placement Support

Dedicated corporate relation team and campus drives securing placement offers from top tech companies (Wipro, TCS, Infosys, etc.).

Coding Culture

Student clubs, competitive programming workshops, and peer mentoring to boost problem-solving skills and project development.

Professional Certifications

Integrated add-on certifications in cloud computing, cybersecurity, and data science to make students industry-ready.

Modern Computer Labs

High-speed networking systems, fully equipped hardware laboratories, and advanced computing resources for research and development.

Why Choose Marian

A heritage of academic excellence, student support, and holistic development.

NAAC A++ Accreditation

Global Exposure

Entrepreneurship Ecosystem
